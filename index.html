Our Story

MADE WITH
LOVE,
SERVED
HOT.

Aunty Momo started as a small stall in the heart of Amar Colony with one bamboo steamer and a recipe that came straight from home. The momos were so good, the queue never went away.

Today we still make every single momo by hand, wrap every fold ourselves, and use the same chutney recipe that made aunty famous. No freezers, no shortcuts — just fresh dough, good filling and lots of steam.
